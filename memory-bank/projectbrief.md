# ThaiPass — Project Brief

## Product

**ThaiPass** — premium visa service for Thailand. Separate brand from the main travel agency.

## Services

1. **DTV Audit** — document package and case audit before consulate submission
2. **DTV Concierge** — full support until visa approval
3. **TR Express** — tourist visa in 3 business days
4. **TR Standard** — tourist visa in 7 business days
5. **Retirement Path** — pension visa 50+ for 1 year

## Geography

- Office: **Moscow** (in-person pickup)
- **Courier delivery** across Russia
- Target audience: Russia + CIS (Wordstat region 225)

## Conversion

- Lead form → Telegram bot notification
- WhatsApp for direct contact
- Telegram channel for news and price updates
- No public phone number at launch

## Blog

WordPress at `blog.thaipass.ru`, consumed via REST API in Next.js `/blog`.

## Goal

SEO landing for Yandex (primary) and Google. Drive form submissions for visa services.
